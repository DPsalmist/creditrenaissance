FAQs

Can I apply and get a loan online?

Yes, you can. You can apply online and we'll get in touch with you. Once your application is reviewed and approved, we'll credit the loan into your account details.

What is the refund period for my loan?

The refund period depends on the amount of loan and your capacity to pay. We usually offer between 6 months and 12 months and 18 months duration depending on the type of loan.

Do you have a physical location?

Yes, we do. Our head office address is stated in the contact us section. We are open from 8am to 5pm, Mondays to Fridays.

Can I monitor my loan or investment?

You can watch and see how your loan or investment fares on your dashboard. It's as simple as logging in and viewing your portfolio.

How much interest do I stand to gain on my savings/deposits?

We offer 12% interest on your deposits/savings per quarter. And you can always reinvest or collect on a monthly or quarterly basis.

What if I can't afford to pay the loan?

We understand the challenges of the country and the economy. We'll work with you to reschedule an easier repayment schedule.

Can I trust Credit Renaissance Ltd?

Yes, you can trust Credit Renaissance Ltd as we are a registered company with the CAC and we have insurance on all our investments.

Can I get a bonus by referral?

Yes, you can. We encourage you with a one time 2% interest bonus on any amount your referral invest with us.
